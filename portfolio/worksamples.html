University of Michigan ITS Internship Website Redesign

This poster explainins the iterations and process used to reach the final product of the redesigned internship website.



To redesign this website, I worked with a team of three other interns to:


  • Perform initial surveying to learn requirements.
  • Streamline the application process using a Drupal webform in place of a Qualtrics survey.
  • Use the Marvel application to create mockups and perform A-B user testing.
  • Convert the site from “dumped HTML” to Drupal framework for easier maintenance.
